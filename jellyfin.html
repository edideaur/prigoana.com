Jellyfin

What is Jellyfin?

Jellyfin is a personal media server that lets you stream movies, TV shows, and more—ad-free and on demand.

Features

- Over 150 albums in high quality


- Over 150 movies in high definition


- Download ANY piece of media


- Last.fm integration


How to Request an Account

1. Follow @edideaur on Instagram
2. DM me with the following format:
Username: your preferred username
Password: your preferred password

Notes:
- Unfollowing will result in termination of your account
- Follow-backs are not guaranteed
- Allow 24–48h for account setup

How to Request Media (Movies, Shows, Books, TV stations, Music)

You can either DM me the title, or use the form at:
https://request.prigoana.com

Client Info – How to Watch

Once your account is active, you can stream using:

Web Browser: https://fin.prigoana.com
Mobile: Jellyfin app (iOS & Android) or Swiftfin
TV: Android TV, Fire TV, Roku, Apple TV
Others: Use server URL with compatible clients

Additional Info

- Accounts are for personal use only. Please do not share your login
- Content requests depend on availability and demand
- DM me if you need help or have issues
- Most music is in lossless quality.
